Background

This form should be completed and submitted every month by:

  • PCNs claiming workforce payments under the Network Contract Directed Enhanced Service (DES) in 2020/21; and
  • Commissioners (CCGs/STPs/ICSs) reviewing and approving the claims
Additionality Rules
  • PCN reimbursement claims must only be for staff additional to the 2019-20 PCN baseline. Where a baseline has not been established, reimbursement claims are still to be for new additional staff recruited during 2019-20 onwards
  • As part of local due diligence, PCNs may be required by commissioners to demonstrate that claims being made are for new additional staff roles beyond this baseline
  • Claims are only applicable following the start of employment and to cover the period of employment (i.e. whilst the individual is in post), providing the employee is not in the baseline as at 31 March 2019
Whole time equivalent
  • For the purposes of this form “WTE” is defined as 37.5 hours in line with Agenda for Change (AfC) terms, but this may vary for non-AfC posts. Where AfC does not apply, a PCN should calculate the relevant WTE according to the normal full-time hours for that role in the employing organisation with reimbursement being made on a pro-rata basis accordingly
Audit and Due Diligence

PCNs are required to keep an up to date record of the following information for their own audit purposes:

  • Signed and dated Contract of Employment;
  • Payslips covering full employment period
  • Evidence of salary (if not included in Contract of Employment or payslip)

Or, in instances where there is a subcontracting arrangement in place:

  • Signed and dated subcontracting agreement with a provider for the provision of the service for which reimbursement claims under the Additional Roles Reimbursement Scheme are applicable;
  • A copy of the Network Agreement – if used for the basis of subcontracting for services/staff;
  • Evidence of Salary for role claimed - invoice / remittance advice / bank transfer
Further information
  • For information on baseline and additionality rules, please see the Additional Roles Reimbursement Scheme section in the Network Contract DES Specification and Guidance: https://www.england.nhs.uk/publication/des-contract-specification-2020-21-pcn-entitlements-and-requirements/
  • The only exception to this baseline, will be those clinical pharmacists transferred from the Clinical Pharmacist in General Practice Scheme and those clinical pharmacists and pharmacy technicians transferred from the Medicines Optimisation in Care Homes Scheme

Please note, as part of their due diligence, a commissioner may request any or all of the above evidence to support a workforce claim
